# Adventure Works Sales Analysis | Capstone Project

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Adventure Works dataset to evaluate sales performance, customer behavior, and regional profitability.

The project follows an end-to-end analytics workflow:
- Data cleaning and preprocessing using Python
- Advanced SQL-based business analysis
- Interactive dashboard creation in Power BI

---

## Business Objectives
- Analyze overall revenue and profit trends
- Identify top-performing customers and products
- Evaluate regional sales performance
- Perform Year-on-Year (YoY) profit analysis
- Detect sales concentration patterns
- Generate data-driven business insights

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- **Python (Pandas)** – Data cleaning & preprocessing
- **SQL (MySQL)** – Advanced querying & analytics
- **Power BI** – Dashboard & visualization

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation (Python)

Using Pandas:
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Standardized column names
- Converted date and numeric data types
- Created calculated fields (Revenue, Profit, etc.)
- Prepared clean dataset for SQL and dashboard analysis

---

## Advanced SQL Analysis

Performed structured business analysis using:

### JOIN Operations
- Combined multiple tables (Customers, Orders, Products, Geography)
- Built relational analysis across datasets

### Aggregations (GROUP BY)
- Regional sales totals
- Category-wise performance
- Customer revenue contribution

### Window Functions
- Running total revenue calculation
- Customer revenue ranking
- Year-on-Year profit comparison

### CTEs (Common Table Expressions)
- Structured complex business queries
- Improved readability and modular query design

### Business Queries Implemented
- Top 10 customers by revenue
- Regional sales distribution
- Yearly tax trends
- Product category ranking
- YoY profit growth analysis

---

## Power BI Dashboard

Developed an interactive dashboard including:

- KPI Cards (Total Revenue, Total Profit, Orders)
- Year-on-Year Profit Growth
- Regional Sales Breakdown
- Top Customers & Products
- Dynamic filters and slicers

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue is concentrated among high-value customers (Pareto effect observed).
- West region consistently leads in total sales contribution.
- Seasonal and yearly trends influence profitability.
- Certain product categories drive majority of profit margins.
- Revenue growth varies across regions and time periods.
